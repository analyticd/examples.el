* xcv

Cut, copy, and paste in the command-line.

** Cut a file:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  xcv x ${input_file}
#+END_SRC

** Copy a file:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  xcv c ${input_file}
#+END_SRC

** Paste a file:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  xcv v ${output_file}
#+END_SRC

** List files available for pasting:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  xcv l
#+END_SRC
