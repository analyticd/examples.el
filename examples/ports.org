* ports

Update/list the ports tree on a CRUX system.

** Update the ports tree:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  ports -u
#+END_SRC

** List the ports in the current tree:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  ports -l
#+END_SRC

** Check the differences between installed packages and the ports tree:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  ports -d
#+END_SRC
