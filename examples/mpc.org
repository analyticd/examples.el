* mpc

Music Player Client.
Program for controlling the Music Player Daemon (MPD).

** Toggle play/pause:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  mpc toggle
#+END_SRC

** Stop playing:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  mpc stop
#+END_SRC

** Show information about the currently playing song:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  mpc status
#+END_SRC

** Play next song:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  mpc next
#+END_SRC

** Play previous song:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  mpc prev
#+END_SRC

** Forward or rewind the currently playing song:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
  mpc [+-]${seconds}
#+END_SRC
